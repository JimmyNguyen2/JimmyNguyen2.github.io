I use the new audio tag in HTML5 to create a MP3 player below.

Now I use JavaScript to add seperate buttons to control the MP3 player. You can play, pause, stop the audio. You can also adjust the volumn up or down; you can use the slider to jump to different time. A "Time Elapsed" will show when you press the play button. When you press pause, the "Time Elapsed" will stop and it will continue where it is when you press play again. If you press Stop then press Play, the "Time Elapsed" will start over from the beginning.
